STEM Resource: Introduction to Biology and Chemistry

For this chapter, we will be working on an introductory guidebook for biology and chemistry, that will be distributed to Junior high students and under. The main goal of this guidebook is to make the students interested in science and offer them an easy and friendly approach to hard and challenging subjects.
