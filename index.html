What is SciForAll?

SciForAll is a student-led non-profit organization that aims to provide more opportunities for K-12 students to learn, investigate, and write about various different science fields. We also focus on creating easily accessible STEM resources for students in junior high and elementary school. We are looking forward to motivating future generations to be interested in science and join the exciting journey of new discoveries.
